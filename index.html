    About

    TGG Group

    Background

    TGG Group is a single family office headquartered in Hong Kong, guided by the vision of celebrating The Greatest Good for all. Guided by our overall vision, we have purposefully assembled a small but mighty team on a mission — TGG Ventures is the financial arm of the Group.

    Our core team previously managed significant pools of capital across Sovereign Wealth Funds, Pension Funds, Insurance Companies, and other global Family Offices. Our journey has been defined by successfully navigating the complexities of global markets across diverse asset classes.

    We actively seek to nurture and enhance the value of our strategic holdings under the brand of "Lit". With an institutional foundation, we remain humble and nimble as entrepreneurs to generate value in all we do.

    Stewardship

    As stewards of the resources we have been granted, we believe it is our responsibility to ensure they are wisely allocated for the most impactful returns — financial and otherwise. TGG actively supports NGOs and charitable causes aligned with this commitment.

    Our primary philanthropic partner is Ocean Vines, an NGO dedicated to ocean advocacy and education. We support ocean cleanup initiatives, environmental education programmes, and conservation events across Hong Kong and the Asia-Pacific region. Because of our ocean's importance to our planet and to future generations, we consider this work a core part of building for The Greatest Good.
