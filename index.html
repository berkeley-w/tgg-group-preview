About

TGG Group

Background

TGG Group is an Asia-based private investment group headquartered in Hong Kong, guided by the vision of celebrating The Greatest Good for all. Guided by our overall vision, we have purposefully assembled a small but mighty team on a mission — TGG Ventures is the financial arm of the Group.

Our core team previously managed significant pools of capital across Sovereign Wealth Funds, Pension Funds, Insurance Companies, and other global Family Offices. Our journey has been defined by successfully navigating the complexities of global markets across diverse asset classes.

We actively seek to nurture and enhance the value of our strategic holdings under the brand of "LIT". With an institutional foundation, we remain humble and nimble as entrepreneurs to generate value in all we do.

Portfolio

Our Businesses

TGG Group invests across four sectors, each chosen with conviction.

Lifestyle

Aap Ba Din

Blends traditional Cantonese cuisine with innovative techniques, offering personalised menus crafted for each occasion.

Verde Vines

A curated collection of exquisite beverages, focused on uniqueness and quality, often investing directly in the Chateau or domain.

Lit by Larry

Where tradition and generations of craftsmanship meet innovation, creating unique visions of jewellery art with creative freedom.

SAGA Lounge

An upscale lounge offering signature cocktails, private events, and vibrant nightlife experiences including guest DJ performances and live entertainment.

LIT Longevity

Premium NMN+ formulas inspired by Japanese longevity science and Hong Kong's demanding standards — precision-formulated supplements for cellular vitality and lasting wellness.

Sports

LIT Sports Global

Using sport as a platform to enhance grit, perseverance, and teamwork, with a focus on pickleball and inclusive sporting culture.

LIT TLP

Hong Kong's premier pickleball venture, combining the longest-running club in the city with professional athlete management under LIT Sports Global.

Media & Technology

LIT Media Group

A dynamic team of creative artists dedicated to discovering talent and shaping the future of entertainment across music, film, and global collaborations.

LIT Creative Moments

A creative production venture under the LIT brand, delivering innovative content and experiential programming.

Alternative Investments

TGGA × Arena Investors

A strategic joint venture with Arena Investors, offering innovative and flexible financing solutions for complex special situations across Asia.

Stewardship

Ocean Vines

An NGO dedicated to ocean advocacy and education. As TGG's primary philanthropic partner, we support cleanup initiatives, environmental education programmes, and conservation events across Hong Kong and the Asia-Pacific region.
